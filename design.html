to start visualizing my website and to do that I needed to figure out where I'll create my temporary Visual
idea. A wireframe to put it simple is a illustration of a page's interface that focuses on prioritization of content,
and intended behaviors and seeing what kind of design you have to your website. As you can see below I have
created a wireframed and the first one being the top left which is my main page, I obviously kept it the same
format I did back in my rough draft just because I thought it was alright didn't look boring but it looked engaging
enough.You can always create your visualize website on google sides or in another program that could be pretty
benefiting to your style without having to worry about things, I continue to go with the same layout of my
